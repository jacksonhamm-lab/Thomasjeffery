The Store

A Calgary store,
with Calgary roots.

Thomas Jeffery has been part of Calgary's menswear scene since 1968. The store carries the names of two sons from one of its earlier owners, Thomas and Jeffery. Today it is family-owned and answerable to the city it has served for over half a century.

What that means in practice is straightforward. We carry the brands we believe in. We fit every piece in-store. We don't sell what we wouldn't wear ourselves. And the people who walk you through a fitting are usually the same ones you will see next time, often for years.

That last part is no accident. Tim Beggs and Jim Smith have each dedicated decades to this store, building relationships with clients, developing real expertise, and doing the kind of one-on-one work that can't be rushed. Alongside owner Len Hamm, they are the people behind the counter when you come in.

Thomas Jeffery is at Kingsland Village. One store, more space under one roof, the same people you've always known.

What We Do

A different kind
of menswear store.

There are bigger stores and there are flashier stores. We are neither. Here is what we are instead.

01 Personal, Not Transactional

We are not a chain. Every fitting happens with one staff member from start to finish. They learn your taste, your build, and what you actually wear. Next time you come in, you don't start over.

02 Honest Opinions

If a piece isn't right for you, we will tell you. If you don't need three suits, we won't sell you three. The point isn't to move stock. The point is that you walk out with something you will actually wear.

03 Tailoring In-House

Our tailor works in the same building, on the same schedule we do. Nothing leaves the store until it fits the way it should.

04 Brands We Believe In

We don't carry a label because it pays for shelf space. Every brand on the floor earned its place because the construction holds up, the fit works for our customers, or it does something specific better than anyone else.

The People

Experienced menswear
professionals.

The Thomas Jeffery team is part of what the store is. Many of them have been here for decades, learning the craft from the people who came before them and now passing it on themselves. They are experienced menswear professionals: the kind of people who can read a shoulder seam from across the room, and who, more often than you would expect, remember what you bought the last time you came in.

Come in and meet them. They are the ones fitting you, picking the right shirt for the right suit, and telling you honestly when a piece works and when it doesn't. That part has not changed in over fifty years.
