01 Knitwear From fine Italian merino to casual layers.
+

Some of the best knitwear in Calgary under one roof. Ferrante and Codice bring Italian-made quality. Refined knits that sit above what you'd find in most Canadian stores. Robert Barakett and Patrick Assaraf are the luxury tee names you'll keep coming back to once you try them. Holebrook rounds out the casual layer end.

In this category: Ferrante, Codice, Robert Barakett, Patrick Assaraf, Holebrook

02 Sport Shirts The casual shirt with a point of view.
+

Elevated casual shirts for weekends, travel, golf, and the occasions that don't need a tie. We carry both long sleeve and short sleeve options. Short sleeve leans more toward golf and warm-weather wear, long sleeve carries further into the fall. 7 Downie St. brings a Canadian perspective on golf and casual wear. DeSoto, Altemflower, and Codice round out the category, from dressed-down Friday to smarter casual dinner. Worth noting: DeSoto reads well in business casual settings too.

In this category: 7 Downie St., DeSoto, Altemflower, Codice

03 Denim Premium denim worth owning.
+

Denim that fits the way clothes should. 34 Heritage is designed with a higher rise and more room where most men actually need it. The cut makes a significant difference. Alberto brings European precision to casual trousers and denim alike. Fidelity rounds out the selection with quality denim built to last.

In this category: 34 Heritage, Alberto, Fidelity Denim

04 Trousers Dress it up or dress it down.
+

Non-denim casual and dress trousers across four brands. Alberto and 34 Heritage carry both their denim and non-denim lines. The non-denim side is where they shine for weekends and smarter casual. Meyer and Bugatti round out the selection with a strong range of fits and fabrics.

In this category: Alberto, Meyer, 34 Heritage, Bugatti

Pant alterations go through our in-house alteration schedule, so plan for a fitting visit and a separate pickup when your trousers are ready.

05 Basics & Essentials The foundation everything else sits on.
+

The things you wear every day, done properly. Robert Barakett makes the case for a luxury tee. The gap between a good one and a great one is wider than most men realize. Patrick Assaraf covers knits and casual tops in the same category. SAXX for underwear that actually fits. Benchcraft, Marcoliani, and Pantherella round out the socks and essentials end.

In this category: SAXX, Robert Barakett, Patrick Assaraf, Benchcraft, Marcoliani, Pantherella

Seasonal · Spring / Summer Outerwear Updated each season.
+

Our outerwear selection rotates by season. We carry Bugatti's full outerwear range, including more formal overcoats that carry over into office wear, as well as Holebrook for lifestyle jackets, and Regency Leathers for leather options. For a custom overcoat, Coppley's made-to-measure program is available through us.

In this category: Bugatti, Holebrook, Regency Leathers

Custom: Coppley Made to Measure

Outerwear alterations go through our in-house alteration schedule. A fitting visit and a separate pickup when it's ready.

Names worth knowing.

A few brands that deserve more than a tag on a shelf.

Shop in shop Bugatti

Thomas Jeffery carries Bugatti's broadest range in Calgary: trousers, outerwear, knitwear, and essentials. If you're looking to build a complete casual wardrobe in one label, this is the place to do it. Stop by to see the full collection.

Carrying: Trousers, Outerwear, Knitwear, Sport Shirts

Canadian brand 7 Downie St.

A Canadian brand built around the way people actually dress: golf, weekends, travel, and the hours in between. Sport shirts, casual knits, and versatile pieces that work across a lot of situations. One of the most consistently wearable labels in the store.

Carrying: Sport Shirts, Knitwear, Golf Wear

Luxury basics Robert Barakett & Patrick Assaraf

Two Canadian brands making the case that what you wear every day deserves to be exceptional. The difference between a standard tee and one of these is something you notice immediately and don't forget. A wardrobe upgrade that doesn't require a wardrobe overhaul.

Carrying: T-Shirts, Casual Tops
